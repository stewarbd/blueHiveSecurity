About

security that
makes sense.

Most cybersecurity guidance assumes you either already understand the threat, or you don't care. There's not much middle ground. That's a gap.

BlueHive exists to close it. We do community work — free education, practical advice, that sort of thing. Written and run by people who work in security professionally and genuinely think the gap shouldn't exist.

Based in Brentford. No corporate backing. Just work we think is worth doing.

How we work

If someone walks out of a session feeling confident and less confused, we did our job. If they feel stupid or talked down to, we didn't.
